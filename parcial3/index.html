BIO

Taylor Alison Swift (West Reading, Pennsylvania, December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter, producer, director, actress and businesswoman.

American singer-songwriter who has received numerous awards and has been wildly popular since she released her first album in 2006. Swift has also released other hit albums like Red and 1989 as well as songs like Shake It Off and Blank Space. His music combines elements of pop, country, and folk. Apart from his music career, he has also made appearances in movies and TV shows. Winner of numerous awards, she is one of the most successful artists of her generation.

About Speak Now Speak Now is the third studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on October 25, 2010, through Big Machine Records.

About Red Red is the fourth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on October 22, 2012, by Big Machine Records.

About 1989 1989 is the fifth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on October 27, 2014, by Big Machine Records.

About reputation Reputation (stylized in all lowercase) is the sixth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on November 10, 2017, and was her last album under Big Machine Records.
